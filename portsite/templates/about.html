I was born in 1998 in a small village town in rural Lithuania.

As a child I used to enjoy farming with family and searching trash cans for bottles to turn in for coins.

From the age of birth until age 8 I was in a foster home, until my dad came and brought me to the United States.

From early age I enjoyed tinkering with computers, and in my teens I jumped into game development on a popular game platform.

This eventually led me to major in computer science, and it’s something I’ve had passion for ever since.

I envision myself to one day be a part of a technology company that will help move the world forward.
